Frequently Asked Questions
What is a temporary email address? ▼

A temporary (or disposable) email is a short-lived inbox you can use instead of your real email. It lets you sign up for websites, receive verification codes, or access gated content without exposing your personal email to spam.

How long does the email last? ▼

Each generated inbox stays active until you replace it in this page. This page rotates to a fresh address after 24 hours by default, and provider-side retention can last longer.

Is this service completely free? ▼

Yes! TempMail is 100% free to use. No signup, no credit card, no hidden fees. The service is supported by non-intrusive advertising.

Can I use this to register on important sites? ▼

We recommend only using temporary emails for low-stakes registrations, trials, and one-time verifications. Do not use them for banking, medical, or other critical services — you won't be able to recover the address.
